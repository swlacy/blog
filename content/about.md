**Hello!** My name is Sid Lacy (phonetic: `/sɪd ˈleɪsi/`). I am a cybersecurity engineer and student in the same field.

> Student?

I am about to graduate from [Western Washington University](https://wwu.edu) – slated for June 2023 – with a [B.S. in Cybersecurity](https://catalog.wwu.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=19&poid=9265) and a [Political Science minor](https://catalog.wwu.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=18&poid=8616). I aspire to, one day, fill a role in my professional life that sees some intersection between the two (hello, CISA?).

I also previously rebuilt, led, and represented the Western Washington University Cybersecurity Club with my good friends from WWU. As a member of the [WWU Cybersecurity Department](https://www.wwu.edu/majors/cybersecurity), I worked as club president with my peers to spread awareness regarding modern security shifts while discussing contemporary news related to the industry at large.

Further, as former captain of the WWU Cyber Defense Team, I managed and promoted activity in several security-related technical events, including [PRCCDC](https://www.nationalccdc.org/index.php/competition/regionals) and [CyberForce](https://cyberforce.energy.gov), as well as trips to conventions such as [DEFCON](https://defcon.org) and [Bsides Seattle](https://www.bsidesseattle.com).

> Security engineer?

To provide myself a career head start after I graduate, I have worked in industry during my time at WWU – most notably, as an intern at AWS. More detail can be found on [my resume](/resume).

> What about non-professional activities?

In my free time, I occasionally write here, populating this site with articles which have a general emphasis on the study of computer security. My intention behind *swlacy.com* is the development of an extracarricular space I can use to enrich my knowledge, providing a more elegant, professional location to publish work, which I can tailor to my specific needs. I also hope what I publish here is useful, or at least entertaining, to others in cybersecurity.

> Enough security – what else?

Beyond security, I enjoy hiking and cycling around the beautiful Pacific Northwest, which I am grateful to call home. I also feel it's important to stay up to date with international and domestic politics – to that end, I can often be found hunched over the news. Finally, when I want to turn off my brain for some time, I also game: I love strategy titles, shooters, and much more.

While this site is not particularly dense in terms of posted content at the moment, regularly uploading new articles is a personal goal I have set for myself. If nothing interests you now, please revisit soon to see new submissions. If you’ve made it this far, thank you! I appreciate your interest in my site, and I hope you find something here that intrigues or helps you in some capacity.

> Contact?

Have questions or comments on my posts? Please, [let's discuss](mailto:contact@swlacy.com?subject=Hello!)! Additionally, I am open to new connections [on LinkedIn](https://linkedin./com/in/lacysw) and [my twitter](https://twitter.com/actuallysid) DMs are always open.

*Have a great day!*
