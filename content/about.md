**Hello!** My name is Sid Lacy (`/sɪd ˈleɪsi/`). I'm a cybersecurity engineer at AWS, and I'm interested in all things related to security, privacy, and technology.

I graduated June 2023 from [Western Washington University](https://wwu.edu) with a B.S. in Cybersecurity and a minor in Political Science.

> University?

While a student, I rebuilt, led, and represented the Western Washington University Cybersecurity Club with friends from my cohort. As a member of the [WWU Cybersecurity Department](https://www.wwu.edu/majors/cybersecurity), I acted as club president to spread awareness regarding modern security incidents, discuss contemporary news related to the security industry, and demo fun projects.

Further, as captain of the WWU Cyber Defense Team, I managed and promoted activity in several security-related technical competitions, including [PRCCDC](https://www.nationalccdc.org/index.php/competition/regionals) and [CyberForce](https://cyberforce.energy.gov), and organized trips to conventions such as [DEFCON](https://defcon.org) and [Bsides Seattle](https://www.bsidesseattle.com).

> Work experience?

I just completed my second internship at AWS, and I've done some IT work/education on the side beforehand. I'm particularly engaged in the vulnerability management and SecOps spaces. I love automating out tedious processes and building new things.

Brief details regarding my qualifications can be found on [my resume](/resume). Want to know more? let's chat – send me [an email](mailto:mailto:contact@swlacy.com?subject=Hello!) or connect with me [on LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/lacysw).

> Non-professional activities?

Outside of security, I enjoy hiking and cycling around the beautiful Pacific Northwest, which I am grateful to call home. Find me after work reading an article, drinking a cup of coffee, or relaxing in the sun – or maybe all three at once.
