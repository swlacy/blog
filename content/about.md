**Hello!** My name is Sid Lacy (`/sɪd ˈleɪsi/`). I am a cybersecurity engineer at AWS and a student at Western Washington university (non-coincidentally, also cybersecurity).

> Student?

I am about to graduate from [Western Washington University](https://wwu.edu) – provided nothing goes horrendously wrong, that'll be in June 2023 – with a [B.S. in Cybersecurity](https://catalog.wwu.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=19&poid=9265) and a [Political Science minor](https://catalog.wwu.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=18&poid=8616).

I also rebuilt, led, and represented the Western Washington University Cybersecurity Club with friends from my university from early 2021 to late 2022. As a member of the [WWU Cybersecurity Department](https://www.wwu.edu/majors/cybersecurity), I worked as club president with my peers to spread awareness regarding modern security incidents while discussing contemporary news related to the industry at large.

Further, as captain of the WWU Cyber Defense Team during the same time period, I managed and promoted activity in several security-related technical events, including [PRCCDC](https://www.nationalccdc.org/index.php/competition/regionals) and [CyberForce](https://cyberforce.energy.gov), as well as trips to conventions such as [DEFCON](https://defcon.org) and [Bsides Seattle](https://www.bsidesseattle.com).

> Security engineer?

I am about to return for my second internship at AWS, and I've done some IT work/education on the side before that. More detail can be found on [my resume](/resume). Want to know more? [Let's talk](mailto:mailto:contact@swlacy.com?subject=Hello!).

In my free time, I occasionally write here, uploading articles which have a general emphasis on security topics. But enough security.

I enjoy hiking and cycling around the beautiful Pacific Northwest, which I am grateful to call home. I like to stay up to date on the news too, and when I want to turn off my brain for some time, I game as well (mostly on PC) – I'm a big fan of real time strategy games and shooters. I'm also trying to get back into reading more, so if you have any recommendations, please [let me know](mailto:mailto:contact@swlacy.com?subject=Hello!).
