Hello! My name is Sid Lacy (phonetic: /sɪd ˈleɪsi/). I am a student and security engineer.

Student?

I am about to graduate from Western Washington University – slated for June 2023 – with a B.S. in Cybersecurity and a Political Science minor. I aspire to one day fill a role in my professional life that sees some intersection between the two (hello, CISA?). I also co head and represent the Western Washington University Cybersecurity Club as a member of the WWU Cybersecurity Department, working with my peers to spread awareness and discuss contemporary news related to cybersecurity. In turn, as captain of the WWU Cyber Defense Team, I manage and promote activity in several security-related technical events, including PRCCDC and CyberForce, as well as trips to conventions such as DEFCON and Bsides Seattle.

Security engineer?

To provide myself a career head start after I graduate, I have worked in the security industry during my time at my university. Here’s a brief summary of all my security-related employment/experience (more detail can be found on my resume):

  • 2022 - 2023: Secure Software Development Project Maintainer for the US Naval Underwater Warfare Center (Keyport Division)
  • 2022 - 2022: L4 Security Engineer Intern for Amazon Web Services (OpSec Department)
  • 2020 - 2022: Computer Information Systems Department Tutor for Whatcom Community College
  • 2021 - 2021: IT Support Specialist for Anvil Corporation

What about non-professional activities?

In my free time, I occasionally write here, populating this site with articles which have a general emphasis on the study of computer security. My intention behind swlacy.com is the development of an extracarricular space I can use to enrich my knowledge, providing a more elegant, professional location to publish work, which I can tailor to my specific needs. I also aim for this space to be of equal use to others – to eventually become a sizable collection of information, fun projects, and more those interested in computer security can use to benefit from alongside me.

Enough security – what else?

Beyond security, I enjoy hiking and cycling around the beautiful Pacific Northwest, which I am grateful to call home. I also like to stay up to date with international and domestic politics – to that end, I can often be found hunched over the news. Finally, when I want to turn off my brain for some time, I also game: I love strategy titles, shooters and so much more. Want to play together? Hit me up!

While this site is not particularly dense in terms of posted content at the moment, regularly uploading new articles is a personal goal I have set for myself. If nothing interests you now, please revisit soon to see new submissions. If you’ve made it this far, thank you! I appreciate your interest in my site, and I hope you find something here that intrigues or helps you in some capacity.

Contact?

Have questions or comments on my posts? Please, let’s discuss! Additionally, I am open to new connections on LinkedIn and my twitter DMs are open.

Have a great day!
